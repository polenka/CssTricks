Welcome to Bright Bite

Welcome to our Family Dental Centre.

Thank you for visiting our website. If you are here then you have taken the first step in choosing the best in dental health. This is achieved by no accident. Led by Dr Ross Stewart and his team, our dentists have the skill, experience and expertise to put you on the road to dental health. By following our advice and visiting us for regular check-ups, we can help you maintain a healthy smile that lasts.

Our Practice

Our dental practice, Family Dental Centre, has been running for over 35 years. Our dentists, Dr Ross Stewart, Dr Peter Craig and Dr James Malouf are here to provide you with all of the help and advice that you will ever need to maintain optimum oral health.

Our two purpose built surgeries provide a variety of different dental treatments. We continue to update our facilities, equipment and techniques to ensure you receive the most appropriate treatments to give you the most pleasing result in the most comfort.

Our Treatments

We endeavour to make sure that all of your dental requirements are comprehensively addressed so you are confident that your visit will be comfortable and beneficial.

We have outlined the various treatments here so you can see what options are available to you. If you are considering dental treatment, whether a simple check up, restorative, corrective work or a cosmetic enhancement, we can help.

For further information on our practice, our treatments or to book a consultation, contact us today at Family Dental Centre, by calling us on 07 3390 6100 or 07 3390 6464 or by using the contact form.
